How To Play

Begin by pressing the play button, after this 16 tiles with different words will appear and a timer will start to count down. You can now click on these tiles to check if they are connected

There are 4 words in each group of connections so there will be a total of 4 groups, To check if words are connected you simply click on four tiles, once you click on a tile they will turn yellow to show that the tile has been selected, once you click the 4th tile the game will check if you have gotten a correct answer or an incorrect answer if your answer is correct the tiles will move to the top and turn green, if your answer is incorrect the tiles will go back to the normal colour

If you manage to get 2 correct connections you will have 3 lives to guess the last two connections.

If your time runs out,lives run out or you have gotten the 4 connections. A single connection will appear on the screen where you have to guess the word that links them together eg Ford,Honda,Nissan and Toyta and the connection would be Cars.

Once you have guess all four links your score will appear and the game will end, you are able to start again and try to beat your score, The highest score possible is 8.

Rules

Each Game will be a maximum of 5 Mintues long

You will only have 3 lives after you have gotten 2 correct connections

Each correct connection is worth 1 point

Each connection is linked by one word
